Sculptris - art should be free

Sculptris provides an excellent gateway into the exciting world of 3D. When you're ready to take your Sculptris creations to the next level for detailing, rendering and integration into even the largest of projects, we welcome you to ZBrush with its host of robust, unique and revolutionary tools.
